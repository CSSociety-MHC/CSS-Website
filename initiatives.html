our initiatives

Staying committed to our goal of empowering all MHC students interested in Computer Science, MHC CSSoc holds various events which aim to cater a wide range of needs and interests of our students. Ideas are first initiated by the organizing team acknowledging existing problems and demands within our CS department or by listening to feedback directly from students and faculty. Events are then carefully planned and constantly revised from our experience and student responses. Topics covered include, but are not limited to, career preparation and orientation, mentorship program, networking with alumni, skills building workshops, and bonding events.

CSS is providing students with a new online mentorship program, Buddy System, where you have the opportunities to become a mentor or a mentee. We aim to build a tighter and stronger community for Computer Science and Data Science at MHC with this remote program as we understand the road ahead could be intimidating. If you need help, the Buddy System will pair you up with someone who has more experience in academic courses and/or internship/research activities. If you want to offer help, we really appreciate you are here!

Navigate tech opportunities like a pro! From tailoring resume to differenting positions, to strategies for technical interview, we offer workshops with recuiters and MHC alums once in while to get you prepared.

Past Events:

Resume Workshop

The resume workshop will take place on September 22nd from 5-6pm EST. It will consist of two portions. The first portion will be a general session where recruiters will talk about different notes that students should keep in mind when writing their resume (i.e. keep it short, note any big projects, etc.). The second portion will be an opportunity for students to meet in smaller groups with recruiters and have their resume reviewed.

Interview Workshop

Ever pondered about technical interviews? Alums from Google, LinkedIn, InterSystems, and ActionIQ will be joining us next Tuesday in a panel answering questions about technical interviews! A portion of the event will be set aside for on-the-day questions. Join us September 29th, 5pm-6pm!

Calling all MHC students interested in computer science! CSS is holding a networking night followed by a Q&A session. If you’re new to CS, come meet other students, visit booths, and ask questions about internships, projects, courses, diversity in CS, and more. If you’re more experienced, network with other MHC students in the department and answer some questions. See you this Saturday, November 14th at 9 - 10 PM EST!
